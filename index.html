Kaza

Kaza is known for its colorful festivals and the Sakya Tangyud Monastery. Rudyard Kipling described the Lahaul and Spiti region as 'a world within a world'. Kaza and the Spiti Valley have strong geographical and cultural similarities with Tibet and Ladakh. The best months to visit are May, July, August and October.

Chamba

Chamba is the land of Lord Shiva which is famous for its natural beauty. It is a small but attractive tourist destination. Chamba is known for its outstanding natural beauty and there are five lakes, five wild life sanctuaries and countless number of temples.
